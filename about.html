Hey, I’m Renee!

My background in Public Policy allows me to grasp a deeper understanding of issues at the core, see how people are impacted at the personal level, and challenges me to be the bridge of communication with like-minded humans.

Right now I’m a Graphic Designer for Levoit, working on the product development team – creating graphics as user-solutions for packaging and the web, and in my free time I have the privilege of mentoring college students & helping them navigate through life.

To grow in my career, I enrolled in CareerFoundry’s UX Design program, learning about the design thinking process from start to finish, and am anticipating to graduate by the end of this year. In the duration of the program, I gained more skills pertaining to frontend development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Some of my favorite things? (1) Anything caprese, (2) the pleasant surprise of heavenly sun rays beaming through a sky full of pllllump white clouds, and of course, (3) a good cup of coffee to kickstart the day. Black, that is.
